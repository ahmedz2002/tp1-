Tiktok

TikTok is a social media platform for creating, sharing, and discovering short videos. Launched in 2016 by the Chinese company ByteDance, TikTok has become particularly popular among younger audiences. Users can create videos set to music, often featuring challenges, dances, and other trends. The app allows for various editing features, filters, and effects, making it easy for anyone to create engaging content. TikTok has significantly influenced social media trends and has expanded its reach globally.
