Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video sharing social media platform launched in October 2010 by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger. It allows users to upload photos and videos, apply filters, and share them on their profile or with followers. Instagram has evolved to include features like Stories, IGTV, and Reels, making it a versatile platform for personal and business use. It has become a significant tool for influencers, brands, and marketers worldwide.
